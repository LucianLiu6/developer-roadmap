Polygon

Polygon, formerly known as the Matic Network, is a protocol that allows anyone to create and exchange value, powered by zero-knowledge technology. Polygon provides multiple solutions including

  • @official@Polygon zkEVM, a zk powered EVM equivalent L2
  • @official@Polygon PoS, a proof of stake, EVM compatible side chain
  • @official@Polygon CDK, a Chain Development Kit for building customizable zk powered L2s
  • @official@Polygon ID, identity infrastructure and SDKs to facilitate trusted and secure relationships between apps and users
