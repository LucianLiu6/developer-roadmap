Signed APK

A Signed APK is a version of your app that you prepare for distribution in the Play Store or other Android markets. When you sign your app using a private key, you authenticate your identity as the developer of the app. It is a required step by the Android system that ensures only updates to the APK that are from the original developer will be accepted.
