Keyword Volume

Keyword volume, also referred to as search volume or keyword search volume, is a metric that reflects the number of times a specific keyword was searched for in a search engine (like Google) within a specified timeframe. This data is typically monthly but can be analyzed over different time periods. A higher keyword volume means that more people are searching for that particular term.
