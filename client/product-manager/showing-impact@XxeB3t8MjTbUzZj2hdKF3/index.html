Showing Impact

As a Product Manager, one of the essential skills to possess is the ability to demonstrate the impact of the product in the market or to the organization. It involves quantifying and presenting the value and success created by the product through metrics such as sales, customer adoption, or even impact on brand, customer satisfaction, or social responsibility.
