Market Segmentation under Positioning

As a Product Manager, understanding market segmentation is crucial in the process of positioning your product. Market segmentation involves dividing a market into distinct groups of buyers who have different needs, characteristics, and behaviors, and who might require separate products or marketing mixes. It helps product managers identify and analyze potential customers, their needs, and how the product can meet their needs.
