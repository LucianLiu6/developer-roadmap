Data Modeling

In AWS DynamoDB, data modeling is a process that involves determining how to organize, access, and understand the data stored in a database. This process is crucial as it outlines how data will be stored and accessed across a wide range of databases and applications. The primary components of data modeling in DynamoDB include tables, items, and attributes. Tables are collections of data. Items are individual pieces of data that are stored in a table.
