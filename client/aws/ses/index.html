SES

Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) is a scalable and cost-effective email sending service tailored for marketers, developers, and businesses. It enables users to send notifications, transactional emails, and marketing communications using a highly reliable infrastructure. Amazon SES eliminates the complexity and challenge of building an in-house email solution or licensing, installing, and managing a third-party service.
