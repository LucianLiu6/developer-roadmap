Common Runtimes

Given below is the list of common algorithmic runtimes. The runtimes are listed in ascending order of their complexity.

  • O(1) - Constant
  • O(log n) - Logarithmic
  • O(n) - Linear
  • O(n log n) - Linearithmic
  • O(n^2) - Quadratic
  • O(n^3) - Cubic
  • O(2^n) - Exponential
  • O(n!) - Factorial
  • O(n^k) - Polynomial
